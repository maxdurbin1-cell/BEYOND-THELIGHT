The M.A.D. System
Roll your Action Die against the Dread Die (DD). Equal or higher = Success.

Critical: Roll the highest number — Explode! Roll again and add.

TMW: Every failed roll grants +1 TMW. Spend 1:1 to boost rolls, or 2 to Push Your Luck.

Trauma: Roll Adventure Die vs Dread. Fail = +1 Trauma.
1–2: Weakened · 3–4: Distracted · 5: Shaken · 6: Vulnerable
Combat
Initiative: Wayfarers go first, then Enemies (2 Actions).
Actions: Light=3, Balanced=2, Heavy=1.
Attack: Strike or Shoot vs Dread. Hit = difference in Stress (min 1).
Defend: Defend vs Dread. Dread higher = that difference in Stress.
Death: Stress ≥ 2× Defend die value.
Recovery: Clear Conditions, halve Stress.
Long Rest: Clear all Conditions, recover all Stress.
Travel & Landmarks
Phases: Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night.
Trek: 1 Hex · Gallop: 2 Hexes · Ferry: 3 Hexes

Wilderness Roll (d6): 1=Random Event · 2–3=Nearest Event · 4–6=All Clear

Holding/Seat: Rest → Protected (Defend ↑)
Dwelling: Rest → Bolstered (Spirit/Lead ↑)
Temple: Rest → Focused (Mind/Control ↑). Sage −1 Trauma
Monument: Pass → Protected. No Wilderness Roll.
Ruins: Rest → Empowered (Body/Strike/Shoot ↑)

Failing to rest in the Evening: +1 Trauma.

Rough Weather: Lead vs DD8. Failure: remain in Hex, lose 1 Phase.
Knowledge & Whispers
Nomads (Dwellings): Know Landmarks and Events in adjacent Hex Zones. Know only the Whisper of an Event.

Sages (Temples): Know 1 random Event and its approximate direction. Know the nearest Landmark. Know a partial Mystery.

Lords (Holdings): Grasp all Mysteries — their secrets, locations, vulnerabilities, and remedies. Know all Locations.

The Sovereign (Seat): All knowledge above, plus can assign Tasks.
Renown & Skirmish
0–2: Unknown · 3–5: Whispered · 6–8: Celebrated · 9–11: Lord · 12+: Sovereign

Skirmish: Roll 2d12 for army Stress. Dread = d6 if ≥13, else d4.
Win a Skirmish for a Holding = +1 Renown.

Trade Routes (d10): 1–2=Safe · 3–4=Profitable · 5–6=Aggressive · 7–8=Obstacle · 9–10=Wayfarer

Haggling: Roll Spirit vs DD8.
Ruins, Temples & Lost Cities
Ruins: 1 Entrance + d4+2 rooms. Rest = Empowered.
Monsters: Beast (DD4|4), Bandits (DD4|8), Builder (DD6|12), Horror (DD12|24)

Temples: d4+2 Chambers. Rest = Focused. Generate rooms to explore.

Lost Cities: d6 Roll: 1–2=Urban Feature, 3–4=Building, 5–6=Discovery.
Irradiated Ones (DD4|8 Stress) patrol. Stay quiet.

Gate Symptoms: Roll Spirit Save on entry.
5–6 = No symptoms or reinvigorated.
